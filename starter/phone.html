Cybersecurity on Your Phone

Smartphones are powerful tools, but they can be targets for scams, malware, and data theft. Seniors should take steps to stay safe:

  • Only download apps from official app stores like Google Play or Apple App Store.
  • Be cautious of texts or calls from unknown numbers.
  • Don’t click suspicious links in messages or emails.
  • Regularly update your phone's software and apps.
