Cybersecurity in Banking for Seniors

Online banking is convenient, but seniors must stay alert to cyber threats like phishing emails, fake login pages, and fraud. Here are a few tips:

  • Always use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Never share your banking credentials with anyone.
  • Ensure you're on your bank's official website before logging in.
