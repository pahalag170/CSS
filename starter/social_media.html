Staying Safe on Social Media for Seniors

Social media can be fun and a great way to stay connected, but it also comes with risks. Here are important tips to keep your accounts secure:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for each social media account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication where possible.
  • Be cautious about what personal information you share publicly.
  • Don’t accept friend requests from people you don’t know.
  • Regularly check your privacy settings and update them.
