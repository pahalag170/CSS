Cybersecurity Tips for Online Shopping

Online shopping is convenient but can expose you to cyber risks like fake websites and phishing scams. Here are some tips to stay safe:

  • Only shop on trusted websites.
  • Use strong and unique passwords for shopping accounts.
  • Use secure payment methods like credit cards or trusted services.
  • Beware of suspicious emails or deals.
