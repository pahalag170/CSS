Cybersecurity Tips While Traveling

Traveling is exciting, but it’s important to stay safe online when you’re away from home. Public Wi-Fi and unfamiliar networks can be risky. Here are some tips:

  • Use a Virtual Private Network (VPN) when accessing the internet.
  • Avoid conducting sensitive transactions on public Wi-Fi.
  • Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches.
  • Be cautious when connecting to unknown networks.
