Insurance & Cyber Security

Cyber insurance is designed to help individuals recover from cyber incidents like identity theft, fraud, or hacks. Here's what to know:

  • It covers costs of data recovery and financial losses.
  • Provides access to legal and IT support.
  • Often includes credit monitoring services after a breach.
  • Can reduce the financial stress of being a cybercrime victim.
